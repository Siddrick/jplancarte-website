Refund Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At JPlancarte Inspections, we stand behind the quality of our work and aim to make every inspection experience straightforward and fair. Please read our refund policy carefully.

We believe in being fair to our customers. If something goes wrong on our end, we will make it right. If you have any concerns about your inspection, please contact us directly at (909) 541-4375 before disputing any charges.

Completed Inspections

Once an inspection has been performed and the OBD diagnostic scan has been completed, the inspection fee is non-refundable. This applies regardless of whether the vehicle passed or failed the inspection, as the service — the inspection itself — has been fully rendered.

Failed Inspections

A failed inspection result does not qualify for a refund. The inspection fee covers the time, travel, equipment, and certified labor required to perform the inspection — not the outcome. If your truck fails, we encourage you to address the identified issues and rebook for a reinspection.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel your appointment, please notify us at least 2 hours before your scheduled time by calling or texting (909) 541-4375. Cancellations made with sufficient notice will not incur any cancellation fee. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a fee to cover travel and time costs.

Inspector No-Show or Error

If JPlancarte Inspections fails to show up for a confirmed appointment without prior notice, or if an inspection is performed incorrectly due to our error, you are entitled to a full refund or a complimentary re-inspection at no charge — your choice.

Requesting a Refund

To request a refund under the eligible circumstances above, contact us directly at (909) 541-4375. We aim to resolve all refund requests within 3 business days.

Contact

Questions about our refund policy? Reach us at (909) 541-4375 and we'll be happy to help.
