DISCLAIMER

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

JPlancarte Inspections is a CARB-certified mobile inspection service. Our inspections are conducted in accordance with California Air Resources Board (CARB) Clean Truck Check requirements.

NO GUARANTEE OF PASS

We cannot guarantee that your vehicle will pass inspection. Our fee covers the inspection service regardless of the outcome. If your vehicle fails, we will provide the specific fault codes so you can have repairs made and book a reinspection.

CARB ANNUAL FEE

The separate $31.18 CARB annual compliance fee is paid directly by you to CARB at cleantruckcheck.arb.ca.gov and is not included in our service fee.

ACCURACY OF INFORMATION

We strive to keep all information on this website accurate and up to date. Prices and policies may change. Please contact us directly for the most current information.
