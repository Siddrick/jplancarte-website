PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

JPlancarte Inspections ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When you submit a booking request through our website, we collect your name, phone number, truck information, preferred appointment date and time, and your location or service address. This information is provided voluntarily by you.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information you provide solely to schedule and confirm your inspection appointment, contact you regarding your booking, and improve our services. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Booking data is transmitted securely and stored in our scheduling system.
