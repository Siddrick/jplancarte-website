Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

JPlancarte Inspections ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you submit a booking request through our website, we collect your name, phone number, truck information, preferred appointment date and time, and your location or service address. This information is provided voluntarily by you.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to schedule and confirm your inspection appointment, contact you regarding your service, and communicate any relevant follow-up information about your inspection results or compliance status.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Booking requests submitted through this website are sent directly to us via SMS or email. We do not operate a database or server that stores your personal data beyond what is necessary to fulfill your appointment.

Communications

By submitting a booking request, you agree that JPlancarte Inspections may contact you by phone or text message at the number you provided to confirm or discuss your appointment.

Third-Party Services

Our website does not use third-party advertising, tracking pixels, or analytics services that collect your personal data.

Your Rights

You may request that we delete any personal information we hold about you by contacting us directly at (909) 541-4375. We will honor all reasonable deletion requests promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at (909) 541-4375.
